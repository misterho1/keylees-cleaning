What I clean

Four services. One standard. Done right.

Every job ends the same way: I walk every room with you (or send you a video) before I leave. If you spot something, I fix it on the spot. No charge.

Standard clean

From $120 · 2–3 hrs

The honest weekly or biweekly clean. Top to bottom, room by room, the way your mom wishes you did it.

  • All floors vacuumed & mopped
  • Bathrooms scrubbed & disinfected
  • Kitchen counters, sink, appliance exteriors
  • Dusting — including baseboards & blinds
  • Trash out, linens swapped (if you leave them)

Deep clean

From $220 · 4–6 hrs

The reset. Behind the toilet, inside the microwave, on top of the fridge, in the window tracks. The stuff a regular clean skips.

  • Everything in the standard clean, plus:
  • Inside oven & microwave
  • Inside refrigerator (on request)
  • Cabinet fronts hand-wiped
  • Window sills, tracks, sliding doors
  • Light switches, door handles, vents

Move-in / move-out

From $280 · 5–7 hrs

For landlords, sellers, and anyone trying to get their deposit back. Empty house, full deep clean, every cabinet inside.

  • Full deep clean, every room
  • Inside all cabinets & drawers
  • Inside all appliances
  • Closet shelves wiped down
  • Wall scuff spot-cleaning
  • Photos sent before & after

Airbnb turnover

From $95 · 90 min

Same-day turn for short-term rental hosts. Fresh linens, restocked supplies, photo confirmation before your next guest arrives.
