Services & flat-rate pricing

Honest prices. No surprises.

Every quote is a flat price you confirm by email before I show up. If your place is bigger or rougher than expected, I'll tell you before I start — never at the end.

Standard clean

From $120 · 2–3 hrs · Recurring discount available

Perfect for weekly or biweekly maintenance. Keeps your house feeling fresh between deep cleans.

  • All floors — vacuumed & mopped
  • Bathrooms — tubs, toilets, showers scrubbed & disinfected
  • Kitchen — counters, sink, stovetop, appliance exteriors
  • Dusting — surfaces, baseboards, blinds, ceiling fans
  • Mirrors & glass interiors
  • Trash & recycling taken out
  • Linens swapped if you leave them out

Deep clean

From $250 · 4–5 hrs · First-time or quarterly

The full reset. Recommended as your first clean, or every 3 months between standard visits.

  • Everything in the standard clean, plus:
  • Inside oven, stovetop knobs, vent hood
  • Inside microwave & toaster
  • Inside refrigerator (on request)
  • Cabinet & drawer fronts hand-wiped
  • Window sills, tracks, sliding glass doors
  • Light switches, door handles, outlets, vents
  • Baseboards hand-wiped (not just dusted)

Move-in / move-out

From $320 · 5–7 hrs · For empty homes

Landlords, sellers, renters trying to get their deposit back. Empty house, full deep clean, every cabinet inside.

  • Full deep clean of every room
  • Inside all cabinets, drawers, pantry
  • Inside oven, fridge, freezer, dishwasher
  • Inside laundry hookups & dryer vent
  • Closet shelves & rods wiped down
  • Wall scuff spot-cleaning (where feasible)
  • Before & after photos sent to you
