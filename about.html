About me

I'm Keylee. This is my summer.

I'm a college student in Utah, and I'm spending this summer doing exactly two things — going to class, and cleaning houses across Tooele and Salt Lake County to save for my first car.

I started this because I needed money, but I kept going because I genuinely love the feeling of leaving a house better than I found it. There's something about walking into a kitchen that looks defeated and walking out two hours later with the counters gleaming. It's small, but it's real.

Every dollar from every clean goes into one place: the car fund. So when you book me, you're not paying a corporation. You're putting one more mile on a college kid's odometer — and getting your house back in shape while you're at it.

— Keylee

FAQ

Stuff people ask.

Do you bring your own supplies?

Yes — I bring everything. If you'd rather I use your products (eco, scent-free, pet-safe), just leave them out and tell me what you'd like used where.

Do I need to be home?

Totally up to you. Most clients leave a code or hide a key. If you'd rather be there the first time, I get it — happy to meet first.

How far do you go?

Anywhere in Tooele County and most of Salt Lake County. If you're outside that, ask — I'll tell you straight up whether the drive works.

What's the 2-hour guarantee?

If you walk through after I leave and something isn't right, email me within 2 hours and I'll be back to re-clean it free. No questions, no charge. That window keeps things fair for both of us.

How do I pay?

Venmo, Cash App, Zelle, or cash. I send a receipt by email after every clean.
